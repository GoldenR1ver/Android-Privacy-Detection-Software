Privacy policy
DATA CONTROLLER 
When you use our application, your personal data which you provide shall be processed by AppMobilEdition SAS, contact e-mail: contact@park4night.com, as data controller. 
WHAT PERSONAL DATA DO WE COLLECT? 
In order to use our app you don't have to register an account. However, if you want to fully enjoy the functionalities of park4night, such as: adding commentaries, adding photos of places, adding new places, you will create an account; In this case the minimum necessary personal data to be provided are: e-mail address, username (created by you, it does not have to be your real name). 
We might collect other information, not considered as personal data, such as: type of your vehicle, preferred language, descriptions deriving from comments and photos. 
Our app uses Google Analytics which is a service offered by Google that generates detailed statistics about our app's traffic. This service is provided by Google according to Google's privacy policy. WHAT IS THE 
INFORMATION USED FOR? 
Your email address is used to: register your account, log in to your account and may be used to contact you if necessary. 
Your username is used to register your account and log in to your account. When you use the functionalities of our app, such as adding places, adding commentaries, adding photos, your username is used to show what you post. 
Your email address can be also used for the purpose of sending you our newsletter, only if you give us your separate consent. 
When registering to our app you can give us a separate consent to receive on your email information about our partners. 
We do not disclose any of your personal data, except username, which is published in the app together with commentaries, places or photos which you post. In the result thereof, your username, as well as places, commentaries and photos which you add are disclosed within the app for the users of our app (registered and non-registered), as well as on our website. 
DO WE DISCLOSE ANY INFORMATION TO OUTSIDE PARTIES? 
Apart from what we have written above, we do not share any of your information, including username and email address, with third parties unless requested by law. 
IS YOUR PERSONAL DATA SECURED? 
We handle the data of our users in a secured infrastructure. We have implemented encryption of transmission when you provide your data over the Internet. 
CHANGES TO OUR PRIVACY POLICY 
If we decide to change our policy, we will post these changes on this page and inform you about it.
